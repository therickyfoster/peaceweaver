### 1. **Peace Psychology & Conflict Resolution**

#### Indigenous Peacebuilding Practices
- **Source**: Anundo, J. (2025). Peacebuilding and Mental Health. SpringerLink
- **Finding**: Traditional justice frameworks (Rwanda's Gacaca courts, Mozambique's Magamba spirits, Ethiopia's Michu system) show significant efficacy when integrated with psychosocial support
- **Application**: Dashboard now emphasizes culturally-grounded reconciliation mechanisms in resource sections

#### Agent-Based Modeling for Scale
- **Source**: Peterson, D.A., Galesic, M., Hammond, R.A. (2025). Journal of Conflict Resolution
- **Finding**: Network effects in peace education can leverage social connections for broader societal impact
- **Application**: Activity feed highlights network-based initiatives and ripple effects

#### Peace Education Effectiveness
- **Source**: Naikare, A. & Kumar, A. (2025). International Journal of Indian Psychology
- **Finding**: Peace education most effective in stable environments; requires adaptation during active violence
- **Application**: Tools section notes context-dependency of interventions

### 2. **Trauma-Informed Care (TIC)**

#### Systematic Implementation Research
- **Source**: Goldstein, E., et al. (2024). The Permanente Journal
- **Key Findings**:
  - 90% of US adults report traumatic event exposures
  - ACEs cost North America $748 billion annually
  - TIC reduces retraumatization AND improves staff wellness
  - Six core strategies: leadership, data use, workforce development, seclusion reduction, peer roles, debriefing
- **Application**: 
  - All tools now cite evidence base
  - Safety-first design principles throughout
  - Resources section links to SAMHSA guidelines

#### Community-Led Approaches
- **Source**: CTIPP (2024). Trauma-Informed Community Change Guide
- **Findings**:
  - Epigenetics shows intergenerational transmission of both trauma AND resilience
  - Community self-determination crucial for sustainable change
  - Paternalistic models underperform
- **Application**: 
  - Dashboard emphasizes local ownership
  - Assessment tools for reflection, not prescription
  - Links to equity-centered frameworks

#### Evidence-Based Techniques
- **Source**: StatPearls (2024). Trauma-Informed Therapy
- **Validated Interventions**:
  - 4-7-8 breathing (parasympathetic activation)
  - Mindfulness practice (emotional regulation)
  - Gratitude journaling (neural rewiring)
- **Application**: 
  - Breathing tool implements 4-7-8 technique with research note
  - Meditation timer cites efficacy studies
  - Gratitude journal explains neuroplasticity mechanisms

### 4. **Community Resilience Measurement**

#### BRIC Framework (Baseline Resilience Indicators)
- **Source**: Risk Analysis Journal (2025). South Carolina study
- **Framework Components**:
  - 6 capitals: social, economic, community, institutional, infrastructure, environmental
  - Place-based adaptation improves accuracy
  - Actionable metrics outperform conventional versions
- **Application**: 
  - Hero stats now reference BRIC framework
  - Research note explains measurement approach
  - Links to NIST standards database

#### Communities Advancing Resilience Toolkit (CART)
- **Source**: Pfefferbaum et al. (2013), updated applications 2025
- **4 Validated Domains**:
  1. Connection & Caring (psychosocial support)
  2. Resources (tangible and intangible assets)
  3. Transformative Potential (collective learning/adaptation)
  4. Disaster Management (organizational readiness)
- **Application**: 
  - NEW resilience self-assessment tool based on CART
  - 4-question simplified version for personal reflection
  - Scoring algorithm with contextual interpretation

#### ARC-D Toolkit
- **Source**: GOAL Organization (2020). 10-year development
- **Design Principles**:
  - Utility and ease-of-use prioritized
  - 30 measures + guidebook + online platform
  - Tested in humanitarian contexts (Honduras case study)
- **Application**: 
  - External tools section includes full ARC-D link
  - Dashboard design prioritizes accessibility
  - Practitioner-friendly language throughout
